A Chef's Exclusive Chicken Curry Recipe



Ingredients You Will Need

  • 800g chicken
  • 1/2-1 coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potatoes
  • 3 large onions
  • 3 stalks curry leaves

Cooking Instructions

  1. First, saute chicken curry paste in hot oi until fragrant. Then, add in the chicken. Continue to saute the chicken until the chicken releases oil.
  2. Second, add in water, tamarind paste, curry leaves, potato, onion, sugar and salt.
  3. Let the chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft and chiken is cooked thoroughly.
  4. Lastly, add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to seperate.
  5. Remove the curry from the heat, and it is now ready to be served.

Enjoy!!!
